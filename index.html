How To Play

Getting Started

  • 1. After entering the game, use the settings button in the top-left corner to adjust game sound, control sensitivity, graphics quality.
  • 1. You can enter full-screen immersive mode using the floating button in the top-right corner.
  • 2. Move with WASD keys and use the left mouse button to shoot, and the right mouse button to aim precisely.
  • 3. Use R to reload ammo, and press the spacebar to jump.
  • 4. The game offers different modes, and you can go online to battle against others.

Advanced Tactics

  • • Use terrain for cover and surprise attacks on enemies
  • • Team up with other players to control resource-rich areas more effectively
  • • Prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle
  • • Know when to retreat and strategically conserve your strength
